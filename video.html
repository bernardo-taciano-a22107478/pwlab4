Milan City

Milan is a cosmopolitan and dynamic city in northern Italy that attracts visitors from all over the world. It is renowned for its fashion and design, historic landmarks, and rich culture. Milan is home to iconic attractions such as the magnificent Milan Cathedral, La Scala Opera House, and the Sforza Castle. The city is also famous for its world-class museums, art galleries, and trendy boutiques.
