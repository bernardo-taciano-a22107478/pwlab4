Milan

Get to know Milan a little better

Santa Maria delle Grazie

Milan is home to the world-famous painting of The Last Supper by Leonardo da Vinci. The painting is located in the church of Santa Maria delle Grazie and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Milan Fashion Week

Milan is also home to one of the world's most famous fashion weeks, where designers and fashion houses showcase their latest collections.

La Scala

Milan is home to one of the world's most famous opera houses, La Scala. Built in 1778, it has hosted some of the greatest opera singers and conductors of all time.

Experience the allure and excitement of Milan, a city where history, culture, fashion, and innovation meet to create an unforgettable journey.
