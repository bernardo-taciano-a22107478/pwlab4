Where is located?

Milan is a city located in northern Italy, in the Lombardy region. It is known as an important financial and fashion center, with a rich cultural and artistic history. The city is close to the Alps, about 150 kilometers south of the mountains, and is crossed by the river Po.

Details The Milan metropolitan area, also known as Grande Milano ("Greater Milan"), is the largest metropolitan area in Italy and the 54th largest in the world.
